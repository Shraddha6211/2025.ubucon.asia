We will be running exhibitions area during the conference. Get a booth in our venue to showcase your to the conference participants.
It will be a great opportunity to meet and network with the participants in person very closely.

# Getting your booth
## Businesses
If a business is interested in exhibiting at the conference, they are required to buy a sponsorship package that includes booth benefit or a booth only package.
Please contact sponsorship team to secure your booth today. You may find the details in the ["Become a sponsor" page](https://2025.ubucon.asia/sponsors/become-a-sponsor/).

## Communities
If a community is interested in exhibiting at the conference, we provide complimentary booths for thoes who applied for.
Please submit community booth application today by clicking the link below.

- [Community booth application](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSembUqDtFDsd_fhADagrert6tT9-r98yzLWz6BuJ7mQkKoaKw/viewform?usp=header)
